Potions Reagents Goodies

Booster Shot

15s - Duration: 36h - Suggested Dosage: 1 shot

A little purple potion in a bottle the size of a shot-glass. Gives a boost to magical and physical stamina, and tastes vaguely of grapes. Wears off gradually after 24h, and can safely be taken repeatedly.

Clearsight

10s - Duration: 1d - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A white potion in an almost perfectly spherical bottle. Temporarily improves vision. Unfortunately, it does not cure blindness-- but it can help with vision that is extremely near- or far-sighted. Tastes like carrots.

Fel-B-Gone

10s - Duration: Permanent* - Suggested Dosage: ½ bottle

Reduces the amount of fel corruption in one's body by a slight amount. Does not reverse/reduce any fel-induced body growths such as horns, glowing eyes, or tails. Tastes like vegetable oil, but is not actually oily in the slightest.

Painacea

5s - Duration: 8h - Suggested Dosage: ½ bottle

A pinkish potion in a flat bottle, suitable for being put into a pocket. Acts as a painkiller that reduces physical or mental pain for roughly eight hours. Unfortunately, it tastes a little chalky, but isn't wholly unpleasant. Available in low-strength or high-strength variants; low-strength is the default.

Stitcher

5s - Duration: Permanent - Suggested Dosage: Varies

A (troll) finger-sized potion, containing a clear liquid. Can be poured on an open wound, and will cause the skin to stitch back together. Mileage varies on the size of the wound. Works on Forsaken flesh as well, but cannot reconnect limbs. Also works internally for any sort of gastro-intestinal injury, but tastes awful.

Quill

5s - Duration: (1d20)h - Suggested Dosage: 1 oz.

A reddish, syrup-like liquid, in a traditional cylindrical bottle. Tastes approximately like fruit. Reduces cough, fever, and other cold/flu symptoms. Makes you feel wide awake but exhausted once it wears off.. whenever that is. Downing the suggested serving feels like you've been jabbed in the throat with a sharp quill, however.

Get-Over

25s - Duration: Instant - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A light purple potion in a standard flask. Relieves the symptoms of a hangover; consists of a mild, quick-acting painkiller and an electrolyte solution for hydration purposes. Tastes like watered-down juice.

Hydro-Bye

10s - Duration: 6h - Suggested Dosage: ⅓ bottle

A dark blue potion in a teardrop-shaped bottle. Water will simply roll off of whatever the potion is poured onto-- and the potion itself will not stain or otherwise discolor anything once poured. Ideal for those that need to take flight in rainy weather, such as bird-based or insect-based races.

Vaporweight

5s - Duration: 1h - Suggested Dosage: 1 mouthful

A blue, foamy potion in a decorative bottle. Causes the drinker to be far, far easier to lift than they usually are. Useful for medical situations where a larger patient must be moved by a smaller healer. Tastes like rainwater.

Focus Fuel

10s - Duration: 5h* - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

An orange tincture in a long bottle. Tastes like sugary syrup, but doesn't have a matching consistency. Helps the drinker focus on a designated task. The task in question must be in mind before drinking the potion.

Frostbalm

10s - Duration: 6h - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A purple potion in an ice-pack-shaped bottle. At room temperature, it has the consistency of syrup, and does nothing. When heated, it becomes more watery, and comfortably warms the drinker's entire body for several hours. When chilled, it becomes more akin to a slushie and cools the drinker's body for the same amount of time. Tastes like tap water.

Tolerance

5s - Duration: 5m - Suggested Dosage: ½ bottle

Allows the drinker to consume things that would normally upset their stomach, such as lactose or other common intolerances. The potion must be taken either immediately before or after consuming the substance in question due to its short duration. Tastes like ginger ale.

Lucky Swig

7s - Duration: 7h - Suggested Dosage: ⅓ bottle

A golden-colored, glittering elixir in a narrow bottle. Tastes like marshmallows. Raises the drinker's good fortune considerably, and makes it easier to find hidden treasures.

Fresh Air

5s - Duration: 1h - Suggested Dosage: 1 full inhale

A bottle with a strange, steaming liquid inside. It is not meant to be sipped from; simply take in as full of a breath as you can of the minty vapor within, and it will clear your airways for a short while. Useful for heavily clogged sinuses.

Artificial Sweetener

5s - Duration: 5h - Suggested Dosage: 1 spoonful

A white, grainy-looking fluid in a round bottle that causes everything the drinker consumes for the next few hours to taste like sugarcane. However, the potion itself tastes like cough syrup mixed with sand. Unfortunately, the potion's effects do not do anything to change this.

Reversal Elixir

10s - Duration: 1.5h - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A purple-and-yellow potion that swirls endlessly, but the colors never blend. Enables those sensitive to Light to be sensitive to Void instead, and vice versa. Not recommended for consumption by certain races due to unpredictable results.

Blush Brew

20s - Duration: 5h - Suggested Dosage: ½ bottle

A cute, pink little potion with an orange ribbon around the neck of the bottle. Tastes like peaches and cream. When consumed, the drinker will feel fluttery and happy, similar to the dopamine rush one can get when they see their crush. Effects are enhanced if the potion is gently heated first.

Foxfire Deluxe

5s - Duration: 6h - Suggested Dosage: ½ bottle

An orange bottle with a red liquid inside. Tastes like cinnamon candy. Drinking it causes you to sprout an orange, translucent fox tail. If you already have a long tail, it makes your current one translucent and extremely warm to the touch. Good for cuddling, or slapping friends with.

Monomorph

5s - Duration: 2h - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A ghostly blue liquid in a darker blue, cat-shaped bottle. When ingested, the tonic causes the drinker to turn into their favorite animal (at a respectable size) for two hours.

Stinkbug

1s - Duration: 45m - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A small bottle with a green fluid inside. Drinking it makes your breath smell like death warmed over. Effect only works once ingested; could be poured into a drink or dish without notice if not for the fact it's so offensively green in color.

Night's Edge

15s - Duration: 10h - Suggested Dosage: 1 sip

A sparkling bluish-purple (or purplish-blue?) elixir that tastes oddly like a fine wine, but without the actual alcohol involved. Upon ingestion, the drinker's perception of the sky will change to that of a star-lit evening, and even feel a cooler breeze wash over their form as if the change in time had been real. Instills a calming effect.

Null'n'Void

15s - Duration: 8h - Suggested Dosage: ⅓ bottle

A black, crescent-shaped bottle with a black liquid inside. Tastes like how sleep deprivation feels. Fifteen minutes after drinking, you will fall into a deep, 8-hour slumber in which nothing can awaken you, not even your own nightmares. You will not forget them when you wake up. But you'll feel rested, otherwise!

Rumble Liquor

1s - Duration: 10h - Suggested Dosage: 1 sip

A chocolate-brown potion that tastes like caramel liqueur. Enables the drinker to purr like a cat. May cause throat soreness with extended use. Will have no effect on those that can already purr.

Bug Juice

1s - Duration: 45m - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A tiny yellow potion that tastes like leaves. Causes the imbiber to have their butt replaced with a random insect abdomen. Mantids, beetles, ants, grubs-- you name it, you can have a butt of it. Why? Why not. Bug butt time.

Berry Good

2s - Duration: 6h - Suggested Dosage: 1 bottle

A little strawberry-shaped bottle containing what looks like strawberry purée. Makes you smell (and taste) like strawberries. Not a substitute for bathing.

Happy Juice

3s - Duration: 4h - Suggested Dosage: ¼ bottle

A clear, round bottle with a sunflower-yellow liquid inside. Tastes like orange creamsicle. Puts a smile on your face for four hours. Perfect for those that work long shifts in shops, or those that just don't want to put in the effort of smiling at someone.

Phaser

10s - Duration: 30s - Suggested Dosage: 1 shot

A ghostly white elixir in a bottle the size of a shot glass. When taken, it allows the user a short window in which they can go through walls or other solid objects of their choosing. Using this to escape prison cells or other means of legal confinement is not advised nor endorsed.
